Barbell Bench Press

Set 1 of 3 8-12 reps

Lie on a flat bench with feet firmly on the ground. Grip the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width. Lower the bar to your chest, then press back up to full arm extension.

Tips for Success

  • Keep your back flat against the bench
  • Drive through your heels for stability
  • Don't bounce the bar off your chest
  • Exhale during the pressing motion

Grilled Chicken Salad

Fresh mixed greens with perfectly grilled chicken, creamy avocado, and a tangy lemon vinaigrette. Perfect for a healthy lunch or light dinner.

320 Calories
35g Protein
12g Carbs
15g Fat
Gluten Free Low Carb High Protein Salad

Ingredients

For the Salad

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup red onion, sliced

For the Dressing

  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • to taste salt and pepper

Instructions

1

Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and your favorite herbs. Grill for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through.

2

Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into strips.

3

In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and avocado.

4

Whisk together all dressing ingredients until emulsified.

5

Add sliced chicken to the salad and drizzle with dressing. Toss gently to combine.

6

Serve immediately and enjoy!

Nutrition Information

Calories 320
Protein 35g
Carbohydrates 12g
Fat 15g
Fiber 7g
Sugar 4g

Notes & Variations

  • Add crumbled feta cheese for extra flavor
  • Substitute grilled shrimp for chicken
  • Add quinoa for extra protein and fiber
  • Use lime instead of lemon for a different citrus flavor
